Frequently Asked Questions

Can't find the location?
  • You can mark the location directly and move on to the next clue if you get stumped.
  • This ensures you keep your game progress and can return later if needed.
  • Use any hints or nearby landmarks on the map to help identify tricky locations.
Selfies at clues:
  • A selfie including you and the clue location provides visual confirmation that you found the clue.
  • Not everyone is required to take a selfie.
  • To advance in the game, you must either:
    • Take a selfie (preferred), or
    • Mark directly if you cannot take a selfie.
  • Choosing “mark directly” still allows progress but does not provide visual confirmation.

Current status of geolocation:
  • The game does not use geolocation or GPS today.
  • In the future, geolocation features will be added to enhance gameplay and clue tracking.
  • When geolocation is added, you may need to enable location services in your browser or phone settings.

Data usage information:
  • Yes, the game currently uses cellular data.
  • Your selfies will be uploaded, and there will be minor traffic to the server for game progress. Selfies will be deleted within 3 days of game conclusion. Contact us if you want your selfies!
  • If you are very data-conscious, you can “mark directly” instead of taking selfies. This minimizes data usage and allows you to advance in the game, though it won’t be as fun.

Co-branding opportunities:
  • Yes! We are developing several game-related activities that can be co-branded for various institutions.
  • This allows schools, organizations, or businesses to use the game with their own branding while leveraging our platform.
  • Stay tuned for details on how to apply or request a co-branded version.

If you have questions during the game:
  • Text Ted Tower at (920) 944-7028.
  • He can provide hints, clarify rules, or help with any issues.
